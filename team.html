THE TEAM

Soham Udar

Team Manager

A dynamic 15-year-old, grade 10 student at Akshara International School in Pune and the CEO of Centauri Racing Formula One Team, a standout in the STEM competition circuit. With a passion for management, go-karting, and watching Formula One, Soham blends enthusiasm with expertise to lead the team. They honed their leadership and entrepreneurial skills in the Dassault Systèmes Young Entrepreneurship Program, where they managed their startup with precision and success, working on aspects like research and development. Soham has also demonstrated exceptional organizational skills as part of the organizing and management committee for Mechathon, a STEM competition for young students at Akshara. Actively engaged in the School ATL management and organizing committee, he was responsible for bringing Formula One to Akshara International School and creating awareness about it. Under their vision and determination, Centauri Racing is poised to achieve new heights in the Formula One For Schools STEM Event.
