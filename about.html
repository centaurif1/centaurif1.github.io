About Us

What is F1 For Schools?

F1 in Schools is an educational initiativ3e created to spark students' interest in STEM subjects by having them design and build a miniature F1 car. This unique program, crafted for teachers with insights from engineers and backed by Formula 1, offers students an exciting and engaging pathway to STEM success, all while having a great time.

Learn More

Our Mission

At Centauri Racing, our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators through hands-on experience in design, engineering, and racing. We strive to provide a platform for students to showcase their skills and creativity while fostering a passion for STEM.

Our Values

We believe in teamwork, innovation, and excellence. Our team collaborates closely, embracing diverse perspectives to create the best outcomes. We encourage creativity and critical thinking, pushing boundaries to achieve our goals.

Get Involved

If you are passionate about engineering and racing, join us! Whether as a student, mentor, or sponsor, your contribution can make a significant impact on our journey.
