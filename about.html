About Centauri Racing

Our journey, mission, and vision in F1 in Schools

Why Centauri?

The name "Centauri Racing" is derived from Alpha Centauri, the closest star system to Earth. After many iterations, we settled on 'Centauri Racing' as it embodies our core principle: to never stop growing and aiming higher. Just as Alpha Centauri shines brightly in the night sky, we aim to stand out in the F1 in Schools competition.

What is F1 For Schools?

F1 in Schools is an educational initiative created to spark students' interest in STEM subjects by having them design and build a miniature F1 car. This unique program, crafted for teachers with insights from engineers and backed by Formula 1, offers students an exciting and engaging pathway to STEM success, all while having a great time.

Our Mission

At Centauri Racing, our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators through hands-on experience in design, engineering, and racing. We strive to provide a platform for students to showcase their skills and creativity while fostering a passion for STEM.

Our Philosophy

"Race Beyond Limits" — We challenge the impossible, constantly innovating and refining to achieve unmatched performance and success. This motto embodies our relentless pursuit of excellence and our drive to exceed expectations in everything we do.

Our Sustainability Commitment

At Centauri Racing, we believe innovation and sustainability go hand in hand. As we push the boundaries of engineering in the F1 in Schools STEM Challenge, we are committed to integrating eco-conscious practices into every aspect of our project.

Environmental Responsibility

We are committed to reducing our ecological footprint through proactive environmental initiatives. This includes participating in tree-planting drives to offset carbon emissions and organizing cleanup campaigns to remove waste from public spaces.

Sustainable Engineering

We actively incorporate eco-friendly materials and techniques to minimize our impact, including eco-friendly paints for our car's livery and sustainable materials for our pit display elements.

Community Engagement

Sustainability extends beyond our own operations; it requires collective action. We collaborate with environmental organizations and actively promote eco-conscious practices within the F1 in Schools community.
