About Centauri Racing

Pushing the boundaries of speed and innovation in F1 in Schools

Why Centauri?

The name "Centauri Racing" is derived from Alpha Centauri, the closest star system to Earth. After many iterations, we settled on 'Centauri Racing' as it embodies our core principle: to never stop growing and aiming higher. Just as Alpha Centauri shines brightly in the night sky, we aim to stand out in the F1 in Schools competition.

Our Mission

At Centauri Racing, our mission is to push the boundaries of speed and innovation, blending engineering excellence with teamwork. We're committed to inspiring future generations through performance, creativity, and collaboration.

Our Philosophy

"Race Beyond Limits" — We challenge the impossible, constantly innovating and refining to achieve unmatched performance and success. This motto embodies our relentless pursuit of excellence and our drive to exceed expectations.
